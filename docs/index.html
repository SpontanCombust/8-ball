8-Ball Pool Game

Use the mouse to aim your cue and left click to place and hit the white ball


Aim of the game is for each of the player to score balls of their respective variant:

Player 1 has to score solid balls, whereas player 2 has to score striped ones.

One exception here is the black balled with number 8

Black ball should be scored by any player by the end of the game to win

Scoring the black ball before all other player's balls means automatic loss.

Scoring the white ball means round loss
